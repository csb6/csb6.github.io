About Me

I am a sophomore student at the University of Arizona majoring in Computer Science and minoring in English.

I am interested in systems programming, game design, computer simulations, and web programming. For the past three years, I have been working with a variety of technologies.

My first two programming languages were JavaScript and Ruby, but since starting college I have gained a lot of experience in Python, which has become my primary language for prototypes and one-off scripts. Starting in December of 2018, I have been teaching myself C++ alongside my studies, and I am currently in the process of making a small game in order to test my skills in the language.

I am also fascinating by logic programming, and any other unconventional programming techniques and tools I can get my hands on. I am always looking for new ways to efficiently write more correct and performant programs.
