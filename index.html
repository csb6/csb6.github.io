About Me

I am a sophomore student at the University of Arizona majoring in Computer Science and minoring in English.

I am interested in systems programming, game design, computer simulations, and web programming. For the past three years, I have been working with a variety of technologies.

My first two programming languages were JavaScript and Ruby, but since starting college I have gained experience in Python, which has become my primary language for prototypes and one-off scripts. Starting in December of 2018, I have been teaching myself C++ alongside my studies, and recently I have been experimenting with the Ada language.

I am also fascinated by logic programming, as well as any other unconventional programming techniques and tools I can get my hands on. I am always looking for new ways to efficiently write more correct and performant programs.
